INFORMATION ON TICKET SALES

UCSD Students (CKI and Non-CKI): We will be selling tickets both online and in-person. In either case, you will be receiving an e-ticket! We will accept credit card (small fee applies), cash and Venmo! Cash and venmo must be done in person. You can also come by our tabling next week and find out how to buy it in person! You can find the online ticketing site here.

Circle K members who are NOT a part of UCSD: Your respective clubs will have representatives to handle all of the ticketing sales for your school. Please do NOT order through the website link posted below. They will be collecting all of the money from you, submitting a ticket order, and emailing it out to you all. This will facilitate arranging rides and logistics. Representatives for your clubs are still being chosen so please talk to your club presidents to find out who they will be. If you are a club president, please check the president's Facebook group, message the UCSD Circle K page, or message president Joseph Le.

All attendees must sign a waiver and either hand it in to their representatives or hand it in to us in person at the event. You will not be able to get in without signing the waiver. You can find the waiver here.

WELCOME TO THE 18TH ANNUAL MASQUERADE BALL!

The 2015 Masquerade Ball Committee would like to cordially invite you to join the UC San Diego Circle K’s 18th Annual Masquerade Ball for a night of elegance, fun, and everlasting memories.

On November 14, UCSD Circle K will be holding its annual Masquerade Ball fundraiser -- all proceeds benefit the Eliminate project. Tickets this year will be $35 until November 7th and $50 afterwards. Attendees will have the pleasure of enjoying the iconic San Diego Natural History Museum in the lovely and historic Balboa Park. This year, we will bring you the Parisian experience with a Night in Paris. Elegance and romance will fill the air as you dance along to the DJ on the dance floor or enjoy the view of Balboa park from the rooftop under a starry night with a live band playing.

OUR CAUSE: THE ELIMINATE PROJECT

With the Eliminate project, Kiwanis International and UNICEF have joined forces to eliminate maternal and neonatal tetanus. This deadly disease steals the lives of nearly 49,900 innocent babies and a significant number of women each year. The effects of the disease are excruciating—tiny newborns suffer repeated, painful convulsions and extreme sensitivity to light and touch.

Join UCSD Circle K in helping the Eliminate Project reach its goal of $110 million! This money goes towards immunizing over 100 million mothers and their future babies. This requires vaccines, syringes, safe storage, transportation, thousands of skilled staff, and more.

How can you help? Visit the Eliminate Project's website to learn more about how you can become involved!

DRIVING DIRECTIONS

This year's event will be held at the San Diego Natural History Museum in Balboa Park.

When driving on 5-South: take the exit for 10th Avenue, then follow the signs for 10th Avenue. Turn left onto A Street, turn left onto Park Boulevard, and turn left onto Village Place. For more driving directions, visit the San Diego Natural History Museum's official website.
